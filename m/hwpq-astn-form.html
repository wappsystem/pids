Health & Work Performance Questionnaire


Most of the following questions have numbered response items. Respond by SELECTING THE CHECKBOX for your preferred answer.
If none of the categories fits you exactly, please respond with the closest category to your experience.
A few questions require you to fill in the numbers or to provide brief written descriptions.
There are no right or wrong answers. Your responses are completely confidential. Please answer as honestly and accurately as you can.
